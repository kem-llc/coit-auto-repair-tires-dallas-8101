Our story

Your Neighborhood Auto Experts Since 2005

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair and tire services that keep Dallas drivers safe and satisfied.

Coit Auto Repair & Tires was founded in 2005 by a team of experienced mechanics who saw a need for trustworthy auto care in North Dallas. What started as a small two-bay shop has grown into a full-service facility serving thousands of customers. Our commitment to transparency, quality parts, and fair pricing has earned us a loyal following.

Today, we continue to invest in the latest technology and training to service all makes and models. Our ASE-certified technicians treat every vehicle as if it were their own, and we stand behind our work with solid warranties. Whether you need a simple oil change or a complex engine repair, you can count on Coit Auto for reliable, friendly service.
