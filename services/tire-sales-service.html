Tire Sales & Service

New and used tires, plus mounting, balancing, and alignment.

Overview

We offer a wide selection of tires from top brands like Michelin, Goodyear, Bridgestone, and more. Our experts help you choose the right tire for your vehicle, budget, and driving conditions. We also provide professional mounting, computer balancing, and precision alignment to maximize tire life and fuel economy.

What's included

  • Expert tire selection guidance
  • Free tire rotation for life with purchase
  • Price match guarantee
  • Improved handling and fuel efficiency

How it works

1

Tire Assessment

We check tread depth, wear patterns, and age to recommend the best options.

2

Mounting & Balancing

Tires are mounted on your wheels and computer-balanced for a smooth ride.

3

Alignment Check

We align your vehicle's suspension to prevent uneven wear and ensure straight tracking.
